About HeLiX

Overview

Health informatics is about using computers to enhance the way health information is processed, and there are three aspects of health informatics that can be identifed: healthcare, information, software. Within each of these areas, there are different documents outlining ethical obligations. HeLiX is an attempt to index and catalog these diverse documents to gain new insights and start new conversations about the area of health/medical informatics ethics.

Publications
